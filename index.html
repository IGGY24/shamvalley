About Sham Valley

Nestled in the heart of Lower Ladakh, Sham Valley is a serene landscape of ancient monasteries, traditional villages, and blooming apricot orchards. This hidden gem offers authentic cultural experiences, breathtaking mountain vistas, and warm hospitality from local communities committed to sustainable tourism.

Culture

Discover centuries-old monasteries, traditional architecture, and rich Buddhist heritage that defines the spiritual essence of Ladakh.

Nature

Immerse yourself in pristine landscapes, from dramatic mountain ranges to peaceful valleys dotted with apricot orchards.

Local Life

Connect with local communities, experience traditional crafts, savor authentic Ladakhi cuisine, and learn about sustainable living.

6 Day Sham Valley Itinerary

Embark on a journey through the heart of Lower Ladakh, exploring ancient monasteries, traditional villages, and breathtaking landscapes.

Day 1

Arrival in Leh – Acclimatization

Welcome to the high-altitude wonderland! Spend your first day acclimatizing to the elevation. Take gentle walks around Leh, visit the local market, and prepare for your adventure ahead.

Day 2

Leh → Nimmu → Basgo Fort → Likir

Begin your journey through Sham Valley. Visit the historic Basgo Fort, explore Nimmu village, and arrive at Likir, where you'll stay in a traditional homestay.

Day 3

Likir Monastery + Pottery Experience + Saspol Caves

Explore the ancient Likir Monastery, learn traditional pottery making from local artisans, and discover the fascinating Saspol Caves with their historic murals.

Day 4

Alchi Monastery + Village Walk

Visit the magnificent Alchi Monastery, one of the oldest in Ladakh, followed by a guided village walk through traditional settlements, meeting locals and learning about their way of life. Experience thousands of apricot trees burst into delicate pink and white blossoms.

Day 5

Lamayuru (Moonland of Ladakh) Exploration

Experience the otherworldly landscape of Lamayuru, known as the Moonland. Explore the ancient monastery and marvel at the unique geological formations that create this lunar-like terrain.

Day 6

Wanla Village Walk → Return to Leh

Take a final village walk through Wanla, experiencing the authentic rhythm of rural Ladakhi life. Return to Leh with memories of a transformative journey through Sham Valley.
